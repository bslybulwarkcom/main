ABOUT US

SLYBULWARK TECHNOLOGIES LLC established in 2012 registered in Dubai Economic department and Chamber of Commerce, we are and end-to-end IT and ELV consultant, Solution provider. We design, implements and supports the ELV & IT systems and provides fully integrated solutions. We are one of the fast-growing companies in Middle East, Africa and Asia. We are also a consulting firm working with project teams from the early concept design phases through to completion and handover stages. We work with Architects, Builders, Contractors, Developers, Engineers and Owners on many types of projects which include Hotels and Resorts, High Rise Residential and Commercial, Retail, Health Care, Sporting Facilities, Institutional, Educational and Mixed-Use Developments.

OUR METHEDOLOGY

We follow in our 5 phases- methodological approach

UNDERSTAND

Understand a business issue/opportunity is recognized and a site survey is then conducted to investigate the business issue/opportunity and a final recommended solution would be put forward. Once it approved, a project plan is initiated to provide these solution.

PLAN

In this phase Project Plan is defined, outlining the actions, tasks & responsibilities, dependencies And timeframes.

EXECUTE

In this phase each actions and task in project plan is delivered and presented to the customer for acceptance, project manager will be positioned to monitor and regulate each task accomplished within permitted timeframe.

CLOSURE

In this phase we list all activities done and documentation of solution and design is handover to customer and we also train and guide them to use the system effectively.

CONTINUATION

Yes, We are happy to provide post project support. We truly believe in customer satisfaction because we value your relation more than your business.

Design Approach

We work as an integrated member of the Project Team and liaises with the Lead Consultant, Project Manager, Architect, Engineers, Interior Designer, Cost and Quantity Surveying Consultants and any other Consultants or Sub-Consultants.

We commence our work by reviewing Project standards and any available design briefs and compare them in context with Design standards used on other similar developments. We combine this with our past and current experience in such facilities as a basis for establishing appropriate design criteria and preparing Design reports, Schematics and Budget Costs. The detailed Design is then develop to the level required for tender and culminates in the submission of Construction documentation to include Specifications, Drawings, Sketches, Details and Costs, as required.

During the tender stage, we provide bid assistance, interviews, review meetings and submit Tender Evaluation reports.

Construction Administration and Site Supervision Services involve site visits to check all installation progress and ensure compliance with the design intent, followed by assistance during the Testing and Commissioning stages to ensure successful handover to the Client.
